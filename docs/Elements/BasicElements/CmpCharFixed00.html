CmpCharFixed00

icon:

 

raises the pressure of fluid. The work done on fluid is supplied via shaft. Component characteristics are fixed or given by external real-signal.

 

Scope/Calculation function

  • Calculation of the change in state quantities of fluid through compressor.
  • Calculation of power or torque required for given pressure ratio.
  • Calculation of pressure ratio as a result of given shaft power or torque.

 

Short description of physical model/calculation method

  • Fluid outlet state quantities are calculated by pressure ratio and adiabatic efficiency input.
  • Causality about power can be selected
    • Give pressure ratio -> power and torque are calculated .
    • Give power/torque -> Pressure ratio is calculated.
  • Pressure ratio and adiabatic efficiency can be input by either parameter or real-input interface.

 

Limitations/Assumptions of model

  • No flow capacity model. The mass flow rate must be determined outside of this compressor component.
  • No phase change of fluid can occur.
  • Simulation of abnormal operation where fluid pressure drops by windmilling or 0 change in fluid pressure cannot be performed.
  • Flow reversal condition cannot be simulated.
